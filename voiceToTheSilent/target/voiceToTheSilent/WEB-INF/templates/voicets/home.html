About us

For years, NGO's have been fighting to deliver aid to the ones in need. From countries ravaged in war and to the ones trying to flee. From entire ethnic groups to single individuals. Despite of all the effort, corruption and disbelief in organizations tend to make people turn their backs to the most needing ones. Our motto is to give VOICE to the ones that really need it. We speak directly with locals and NGO'S that operate locally and worldwide, cutting middle men and creating a collective, with more transparency.
